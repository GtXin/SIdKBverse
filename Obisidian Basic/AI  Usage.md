# AI & Codex Integration

## Purpose

AI is not the knowledge system.

Obsidian is not the knowledge system.

The knowledge system is the combination of:

- Knowledge
- Processes
- Standards
- Human Judgment

AI exists to accelerate learning, organization, retrieval, and synthesis.

# Core Philosophy

## AI Should Process, Humans Should Decide

AI is good at:

- Summarization
- Extraction
- Organization
- Drafting
- Pattern Recognition

- Judgment
- Prioritization
- Strategy
- Experience
- Original Insight

### Principle

> AI generates possibilities.
>
> Humans determine value.

# Reading Workflow

## Phase 1: AI Preview

Before reading a chapter or book:

Ask AI:

- What are the most important concepts?
- What are the key applications?
- What are the key mental models?
- What are the 20% of ideas that explain 80% of the content?
- What should I pay attention to while reading?

### Goal

Create a mental map before reading.

---

## Phase 2: Reading

Read the source material.

Do not blindly trust the AI summary.

Compare:

- What AI highlighted
- What I think is important

### Goal

Develop understanding and independent judgment.

---

## Phase 3: AI Processing

After reading:

Provide chapter scans, PDFs, or source material.

Ask AI to generate:

- Chapter Summary
- Key Concepts
- Definitions
- Applications
- Suggested Notes
- Suggested Links

### Goal

Reduce manual note-taking effort.

---

## Phase 4: Human Review

Review all AI-generated content.

Add:

### Why I Care

- Personal relevance
- Business relevance
- Research relevance
- Practical applications

### My Thoughts

- Agreements
- Disagreements
- Questions
- Insights

### Goal

Transform information into understanding.

## Knowledge

> What do I know?

- Accommodation

- Economics

---

## Operations

Answers:

> How do I work?

Examples:

- Workflows
- Templates
- Standards
- Agent Instructions

---

# AGENTS.md

Purpose:

Define how AI agents should behave.

Example:

```text
When processing textbooks:

1. Generate chapter summary.
2. Extract top concepts.
3. Extract applications.
4. Suggest concept notes.
5. Suggest links.
6. Never create permanent notes automatically.
7. Human approval required.
```

### Principle

AGENTS.md does not directly control AI.

It provides operational guidance.

---

# STANDARDS.md

Purpose:

Define formatting and organizational standards.

Example:

```text
Every concept note should contain:

- Definition
- Summary
- Applications
- Why I Care
- Related Notes
```

### Principle

Consistency reduces friction.

---

# WORKFLOWS.md

Purpose:

Define repeatable processes.

Examples:

- Textbook Workflow
- Paper Review Workflow
- Book Processing Workflow

### Principle

Codify successful behavior.

---

# TEMPLATES.md

Purpose:

- Research Paper Template

### Principle

Reduce repetitive work.

---

# PHILOSOPHY.md

Capture foundational beliefs.
